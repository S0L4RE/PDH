Social Health

Your social health is important as not only does it encourage you to go outside and stay active but it also helps with your mental health. Which is incredibly important as being social can help prevent/reduce depresstion which has serious effects on every aspect of your health. Being socially healthy can also make you happier as you are more likely to make new friends. Your also likely to learn things you otherwise woudn't have learnt otherwise from being around diffrent people.

Suggestions to devolop your social skills

Maintaining your social health can be hard for a lot of people perticaly if they arent invited to stuff outside of school or if they don't have many friends. Luckily its fairly easy to overcome this challenge just ask your friends if they want to hang out after school. This is great as if you hang out with them a couple of times outside of school they are likely to think about inviting you next time they are doing something. Suddenly you will find yourself regularyly invited to stuff and meeting new people finding new friends.
